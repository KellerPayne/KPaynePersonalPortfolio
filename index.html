About Me

My name is Keller Payne. I am 30 years old. And I am a senior Computer Science student at the University of New Orleans. My expected graduation date is December 2025.

After being introduced to coding while handling my school's website, I found a passion in the intricate details of different coding languages, and eventually enrolled at Delgado Community College in May of 2023 to begin fulfilling undergraduate coursework for a degree in Computer Science that were not fulfilled with my teaching degree. In December of 2023, I left teaching after 7 years in the classroom to enroll full time at the University of New Orleans.

I have maintained a 4.0 GPA and intend on continuing to do so throughout the duration of my senior year.
